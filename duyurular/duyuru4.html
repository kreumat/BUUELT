Workshop Details

Professor Smith will be hosting a special workshop on modern language teaching methodologies. Open to all members interested in pedagogical approaches. The workshop will cover communicative language teaching, task-based learning, and the latest research in language acquisition.

Limited spots available - register by June 25th!
